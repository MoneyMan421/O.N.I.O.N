# Code of Conduct

## 🎯 Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open, safe, and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the O.N.I.O.N project a harassment-free, respectful experience for everyone.

This is especially important given O.N.I.O.N's mission to protect children and vulnerable users. We hold ourselves and our community to the highest standards of conduct.

## 🌟 Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- **Respectful Communication:** Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Constructive Feedback:** Providing and accepting graceful, specific feedback
- **Empathy:** Showing empathy toward other community members
- **Focus on Safety:** Prioritizing safety, security, and accountability in all discussions
- **Transparency:** Being honest and clear about intentions and constraints
- **Child-First Thinking:** Considering child safety implications in all contributions

### Unacceptable Behaviors

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or deliberate intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Proposing changes that compromise safety or security
- Attempting to bypass or circumvent verification layers
- Advocating for reduced accountability or transparency
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a child-safe environment
- Other conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## 🛡️ Safety-First Principles

Given O.N.I.O.N's focus on child safety:

1. **Always Consider Safety Impact:** Before proposing changes, consider safety implications
2. **Default to More Verification:** When in doubt, add checks rather than remove them
3. **Transparency Over Convenience:** Never sacrifice explainability for efficiency
4. **Accountability is Non-Negotiable:** All actions must be auditable

## 👥 Our Responsibilities

### Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior
- Removing, editing, or rejecting contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct
- Maintaining the integrity of the 9-layer verification architecture

### Contributors

Contributors are responsible for:
- Adhering to this Code of Conduct in all project spaces
- Respecting the verification requirements at all layers
- Reporting unacceptable behavior to project maintainers
- Prioritizing safety and accountability in contributions

## 📢 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project repositories and branches
- Issue trackers, pull requests, and discussions
- Official project social media and communication channels
- Project events and meetups (virtual or in-person)
- Any space where someone represents the O.N.I.O.N project

## 🚨 Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:
- Opening a confidential issue with project maintainers
- Contacting the project team directly at [INSERT CONTACT EMAIL]

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

### Process

1. **Report Received:** All reports are taken seriously and reviewed promptly
2. **Investigation:** Maintainers will investigate and determine appropriate response
3. **Action:** Consequences may include:
   - Warning
   - Temporary ban from project spaces
   - Permanent ban from project participation
4. **Appeal:** Decisions may be appealed to the project leadership

### Confidentiality

All reports will be kept confidential. Reporter information will not be shared without explicit permission, except as required by law.
